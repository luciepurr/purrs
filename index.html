Welcome to Lucie's Purrs!

If you've found your way here, you're probably a fellow cat lover, or at least use cute animal pictures to brighten up your day, so excellent choice. This site is a labor of love, dedicated to my cats, and a desire to share them with anyone anywhere who will listen - all the better when willingly!

While many posts and pictures will be current, I have several years worth - decades, really - of images, videos, and stories never told outside my circle of friends, that will find their place here when they come across my mind, including cats here and since gone. I only hope you enjoy the pictures and stories I can share, whether from yesterday or yesteryear, and just maybe, they can live on a little longer in everyone else's hearts too.
